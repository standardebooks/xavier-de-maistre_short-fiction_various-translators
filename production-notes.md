John Andrews also included a translation of "A Journey Round My Room" in his volume, but the SE producer considered his translation to be inferior to Attwell's, so Attwell's was used instead. The headers were removed from "Journey" for consistency with "Night Journey" (and they are not present in the original French), and in "Night Journey" the dog's name was changed to match Attwell's translation, for consistency. The original translation of "The Young Siberians" was titled "Prascovia Lopouloff" by the translator, but this was changed by the S.E. Editor to match De Maistre's actual title.
